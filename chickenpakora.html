Chicken Pakora

Chicken Pakora is an all time favorite and loved snack accross India.

This popular dish is made with boneless chicken and a marinade of besan, rice flour, cornflour, and egg.

This dish has a nice mix spice and savor. This appetizer is a household favorite.
